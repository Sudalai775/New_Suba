About Shree Shuba Associates

Our Story

Established in 2010, Shree Shuba Associates was founded by Mr. A.C.S. Mutho, who brings over 26 years of domain knowledge in international global logistics. Our company has grown from a single office in Chennai to multiple locations across India including Tuticorin, Sricity, Krishnapatnam port, and Mumbai, with plans to expand to Pune.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional freight forwarding and consultancy services while mentoring new exporters/importers through the complexities of global trade.

Our Services

Comprehensive logistics solutions tailored to meet your specific business needs

Ocean Freight
